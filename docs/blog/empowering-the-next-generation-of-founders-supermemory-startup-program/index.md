If there’s one thing we’ve learned while building Supermemory, it’s that most startups don’t fail because they didn't build features; they fail when infrastructure slows them down, or they built too slow.

Modern AI teams are forced to solve the same problem again and again:  
how to make their products remember context reliably, affordably, and at scale.

We’ve lived that journey ourselves, and along the way, we were supported by founders, friends from the community, mentors, and early customers who believed in us and helped us stay focused on the mission.

Today, we’re proud to give back to the community that built us.

## Introducing the Supermemory Startup Program

Our startup program is designed for early-stage founders who want to ship real products quickly, without rebuilding memory infrastructure from scratch.

### What you get:

- $1,000 of supermemory usage
- dedicated assistance from our team
- fast onboarding and simple setup
- 6 months to build, iterate, and launch

We're starting this initiative with a simple goal of removing “memory complexity” as a blocker, so founders can focus on building products people love.

We’ve already seen early teams ship in days instead of months because Supermemory handled context, recall, and persistence for them. This program makes that acceleration available to anyone starting outcreating that same momentum for the next generation of builders.

### Why are we doing this?

- Great ideas shouldn’t be gated behind infrastructure costs or months of engineering lift
- We believe founders should be able to test ideas quickly
- AI products should remember without any hacks
- Teams shouldn’t have to maintain custom memory systems forever
- And we know what a difference it makes when someone supports at the right moment

The Startup Program is our contribution to that future where founders build tools that truly understand users, grow with them, and don’t forget.

If you’re an early-stage company or about to become one, we’d love to support you.

We can’t wait to see what you build.
